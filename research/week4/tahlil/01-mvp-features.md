# MVP Feature Prioritizatsiyasi (RICE)

**Metod:** RICE = Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort (barchasi 1-10)
- Reach: 1=10 user, 5=100+, 10=1000+
- Impact: 1=kichik, 5=muhim, 10=hal qiluvchi
- Confidence: 1=33% taxmin, 5=67%, 10=95% data
- Effort: 1=2+ oy, 5=2-4 hafta, 10=1 hafta
