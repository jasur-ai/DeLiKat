## B2C vs B2B: NEGA B2B?

| Mezon | B2C | B2B | DeLiKet |
|-------|-----|-----|---------|
| Marketing xarajati | $50K+/oy | $5-10K/oy | ✅ B2B |
| Bitim o'rtacha qiymati | $20-100 | $500-5,000 | ✅ B2B |
| Break-even | 18-24 oy | 6-12 oy | ✅ B2B |
| O'zbekistonga mos | Qiyin | Qulay | ✅ B2B |

> **Strategik qaror:** B2C marketing xarajati O'zbekiston startapi uchun juda yuqori. B2B tarmoq effekti tez, past xarajat, yuqori bitim qiymati.
